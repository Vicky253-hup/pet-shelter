About Pawsitively Yours

Pawsitively Yours is India's leading pet adoption platform dedicated to connecting loving families with adorable pets in need of forever homes. Our mission is to reduce pet homelessness and promote responsible pet ownership across India.

Founded in 2023, we've successfully facilitated over 5,000 adoptions nationwide, partnering with animal shelters, rescuers, and veterinary clinics in major Indian cities including Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, and Chennai.

Every pet on our platform is health-checked, vaccinated, and spayed/neutered before being made available for adoption. We also provide post-adoption support to ensure successful transitions for both pets and families.
